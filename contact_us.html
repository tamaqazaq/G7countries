Frequently Asked Questions

What is the G7?

The Group of Seven (G7) is an intergovernmental political and economic forum consisting of Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

How often does the G7 meet?

The G7 leaders meet annually at the G7 Summit, while other officials meet throughout the year.

What are the main topics discussed at G7 summits?

Key topics include trade, security, economics, and climate change.
